Family relocation requires more than a single application. Timelines, relationship documents, housing plans, schooling needs, and the main pass holder's status can all affect preparation.

## Confirm eligibility before collecting documents

Begin with the sponsor, relationship, and intended length of stay. That keeps the document checklist focused and avoids unnecessary back-and-forth.

## Keep records consistent

Names, dates, and relationship details should be consistent across passports, certificates, translations, and application materials.

## Ask early about follow-on needs

Families may also need guidance on Dependant's Pass matters, renewals, or future Permanent Residency planning.
