Singapore work pass planning usually starts with the role, salary, candidate profile, and employer context. A strong preparation process is less about chasing a single form and more about presenting the facts clearly.

## Start with the purpose of the move

An Employment Pass pathway is typically considered for foreign professionals, managers, executives, and specialists. S Pass support may be relevant for mid-skilled roles where quota, levy, and salary requirements also matter.

## Prepare the employer and candidate materials together

Employers should be ready with company details, role scope, compensation information, and supporting business context. Candidates should prepare passport details, education records, employment history, and any professional credentials.

## Plan for family needs early

If a spouse or children are relocating, related pass planning should be discussed at the same time. That keeps timelines, documents, and expectations aligned.
